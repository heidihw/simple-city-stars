This app provides simple astronomical forecasts for people interested in watching the stars. Select for which locations you want forecasts, for which days you want sunrise and sunset forecasts, and for which days you want astronomical viewing condition forecasts.

In the options below, the values for Days and Hours represent for how many units of time out from the present time the conditions will be forecasted. For example, 0 Days will give the sunrise and sunset forecasts for today, and 1 Days for tomorrow. Likewise, 3 Hours will give the astronomical viewing condition forecasts for 3 hours from the end of the current hour, and 24 Hours for 1 full day from the present time.

Please select at least one of each option to receive a forecast.
